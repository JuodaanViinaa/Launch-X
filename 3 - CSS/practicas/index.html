Why should I get vaccinated?

In order to avoid any doubts about getting the COVID-19 vaccine, identify the following 4 benefits of vaccination:

Minimize the spread of viruses

The vaccine has been proven effective to prevent people from getting infected with COVID

Forming antibodies

The COVID vaccine helps shape the antibody response for the immune system

Protecting people nearby

You can also protect those who matter the most to you, and those who cannot get the vaccine

Creating herd immunity

When enough people are vaccinated, those who are not will also be protected by group or herd immunity

Preparations before the vaccine

The success of vaccines is strongly influenced by the strength of the body's immune system. Therefore, you can try the following advice to boost its effectiveness:

Avoid alcoholic beverages

Avoid stress

Eat healthy food

Get enough sleep

Exercise
